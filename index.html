Concept of pharmaceutical care

Definition of pharmaceutical care

It is the responsible provision of drug therapy to produce a definite outcome that will improve a patient's quality of life.


Outcomes of Therapy

  • Clinical outcome
  • Economic outcome
  • Humanistic outcome

Steps in pharmaceutical care

  1. Establish a professional or therapeutic relationship
  2. collect patient's specific subjective and objective data
  3. Evaluate data
  4. Develop and implement a care plan
  5. Monitor outcome
  6. Documentation

Importance of pharmaceutical care

  • Reduction in the cost of healthcare
  • Reduction in the frequency of hospital visitation
  • Improvement of the profession's image
  • Increased adherence
  • Prolongation of life
